How it works

From field to donor
in one system

01 📱

Field Staff Collect

Staff use the ReportBase Android app to fill forms, capture GPS location, photos, and signatures — online or offline.

02 🏢

Admins Manage

Organisation admins review submissions, manage staff, build custom forms, and monitor field activity from the Client Panel.

03 📊

Donors & Stakeholders View

Funders access the Report Portal to see live trends, impact summaries, maps, and automated email reports — no login needed from staff.

04 🤖

AI Reviews Quality

Optional AI review engine checks submission quality and flags anomalies — so bad data never reaches your reports.

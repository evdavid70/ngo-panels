How it works

From field to insight
in one system

01 🏢

Admins Setup

Organisation admins build custom forms, manage staff and departments, assign field tasks, and control everything from the Client Panel.

02 📱

Field Staff Collect

Staff use the ReportBase Android app to fill forms, capture GPS location, photos, and signatures — works offline and syncs when connected.

03 📈

Comprehensive Reporting

ReportBase automatically organises all field data into visual reports — trends, maps, GPS tracking, staff scorecards, and impact summaries. No spreadsheet setup, no database — it just works.

04 🤖

AI Reviews Quality

Optional AI review engine checks submission quality and flags anomalies — so bad data never reaches your reports.

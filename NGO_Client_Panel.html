🤖 Gemini AI Key

Your organisation's Gemini AI key is stored securely on the server — it is never sent to any device. Staff use AI features automatically once a key is saved here.

Get a free key at aistudio.google.com/app/apikey

Paste your full Gemini API key. It will be stored encrypted on the server against your organisation name only — not shared with any other organisation.
ℹ️ How it works
• Your key is stored on the server — staff devices never see it.
• When a staff member submits a form with AI enabled, the app sends the text to the server. The server calls Gemini using your key and returns the result.
• Removing the key disables AI for all staff in your organisation immediately.
• If you change your key, just paste the new one and click Save — it overwrites the old one.

Install the App

1
Open Google Play Store on your Android phone

Search for ReportBase or ask your admin to share the direct link.

2
Tap Install

The app is free to download. No payment required.

3
Open the app once installed

You will see the setup screen — ready for your first scan.

✅ ReportBase requires Android 8.0 or later. Most phones from 2018 onwards are supported.
🔑

First-Time Setup

You only do this once — when you first install the app or get a new phone.

1
Scan the Organisation QR code

Your admin will give you a QR code — printed or sent via WhatsApp. Tap Scan QR in the app and point your camera at it.

2
Enter your username and password

Your admin gives you these. The username is usually your name or initials (e.g. john.field).

3
Set up fingerprint login (recommended)

The app will ask if you want to use your fingerprint. Tap Enable — this means you never type your password again.

4
Set a 4-digit PIN as backup

Choose any 4 digits you will remember. Used when fingerprint is not available.

5
You are in — your forms will appear

The app syncs your assigned forms automatically. This takes a few seconds on first load.

⚠️ Keep your password safe. Do not share it with anyone — not even your admin. If you forget it, ask your admin to reset it for you.
👆

Daily Login

After first setup, you never enter your password again. Every day works like this:

Login
👆
Touch to login
Use your fingerprint
or enter PIN
Open app → instant access

Fingerprint login

Open the app → fingerprint prompt appears → touch sensor → you're in. Takes less than 2 seconds.

PIN login

If fingerprint fails or is unavailable → tap Use PIN → enter your 4 digits → you're in.

After signing out

The fingerprint/PIN prompt appears again — not the full login form. You don't need to scan the QR code again.

ℹ️ The full login form (with username + password) only appears if your device data was wiped or you reinstalled the app. In that case, ask your admin for the organisation QR code to set up again.
📋

Finding Your Forms

After login you land on the Staff Dashboard. Your forms are listed here.

Why do I only see some forms?

Your admin assigns forms to departments. You see only the forms for your department. If a form is missing, ask your admin to check your department assignment.

Forms not showing up?

The app needs an internet connection to sync new forms. Connect to WiFi or mobile data, wait a few seconds, then pull down to refresh the dashboard.

External forms

Some organisations use forms imported from external sources. These appear in a separate section on your dashboard, labelled External Forms.

✍️

Filling a Form

1
Tap the form name

From your dashboard, tap the form you want to fill.

2
Fill each field

Work through the fields one by one. Required fields are marked — you cannot submit until they are filled.

3
For photo fields — tap to take or pick a photo

The camera opens. Take a new photo or choose from your gallery.

4
For GPS fields — tap to capture location

The app records your current coordinates automatically. Make sure location is enabled on your phone.

5
For signature fields — draw your signature

Use your finger to sign directly on screen. Tap Clear to redo.

✅ Your answers are saved automatically as you go. If you close the app mid-way, your progress is kept — just reopen the form to continue.
📤

Submitting a Form

1
Fill all required fields

The Submit button stays grey until all required fields are complete.

2
Tap Submit

Your submission is saved to the phone instantly — even without internet.

3
The app syncs when connected

When your phone has internet, the submission is automatically sent to your organisation. You will see a sync indicator in the dashboard.

ℹ️ Once submitted, you cannot edit a form. If you made a mistake, let your admin know — they can delete the submission and you can resubmit.
📶

Working Offline

No internet? No problem.

ReportBase is designed for field work. You can fill and submit forms without any internet connection. The app stores everything on your phone and syncs automatically when you reconnect.

What works offline

  • Opening and filling any form
  • Taking photos and capturing GPS
  • Submitting forms (saved to phone)
  • Viewing your past submissions
  • PIN and fingerprint login

What requires internet

  • Syncing new forms from your admin
  • Uploading submissions to the server
  • Checking for app updates
✅ When you return from the field and connect to WiFi, all pending submissions upload automatically. You do not need to do anything manually.

PIN & Fingerprint

Changing your PIN

1
Go to Settings → Security
2
Tap PIN Login → Change PIN

Enter your current PIN, then set a new one.

Turning fingerprint on or off

1
Go to Settings → Security
2
Tap the Fingerprint Login toggle

Green = ON. Grey = OFF. Your phone must support fingerprint for this to appear.

⚠️ If you remove your PIN and disable fingerprint, you will need to enter your full username and password each time you open the app.

Language

ReportBase supports 13 languages. You can switch at any time from Settings.

Changing the app language

1
Go to Settings → Language
2
Select your language from the list

The app restarts and displays in the chosen language.

Supported languages

English · اردو · پښتو · سنڌي · سرائیکی · بلوچی · বাংলা · Français · Nederlands · हिन्दी · العربية · Kiswahili · Português

ℹ️ For Urdu, Saraiki, and Balochi — the app uses the Nastaleeq font for correct right-to-left display.

Change Password

1
Go to Settings → Change Password
2
Enter your current password
3
Enter your new password twice

Both entries must match.

4
Tap Save

Your new password is active immediately.

⚠️ If you forget your current password, you cannot change it yourself. Ask your admin to reset it from the Client Panel.

FAQ

I scanned the QR but nothing happened

Make sure you are scanning the correct QR code — the one your admin gave you, not a random one. The QR must be for your organisation. Try moving closer or improving the lighting.

My forms disappeared

This usually means your department assignment changed in the admin panel. Connect to the internet and pull down to refresh. If forms are still missing, contact your admin.

The app shows a TRIAL banner

This is your organisation's licence status — not something you can change. Let your admin know so they can sort it from their end.

My submission is stuck on Pending

The app needs internet to upload. Connect to WiFi or mobile data. The sync happens automatically — you don't need to tap anything. If it stays stuck after reconnecting, try closing and reopening the app.

I got a new phone — do I start over?

Yes — install the app, then ask your admin to reset your device. They do this from the Client Panel. Once reset, scan the organisation QR code again and log in. Your past submissions are safe on the server.

Who do I contact for help?

First contact your organisation admin — they manage your account. For technical issues with ReportBase itself, your admin can reach out to hello@reportbase.app.

Need more help? Contact your organisation admin or email hello@reportbase.app
